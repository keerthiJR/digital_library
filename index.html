DIGITAL GALLERY

Sudharsana Jwala digital library (SJ digital library) is an online database consisting of the 3D models, 2D sketches of the icons, Dyana slogams, audio and video. SJ digital library serves as the reference for those who are interested to know more about the iconography. Based on the observations from the Shilpashastra, we have classified the deity forms into ten distinct categories. This will serve as a platform for the next generation artisans and enthusiasts to learn about the icons and forms.
